CHECKLIST

The checklist below will let you know what foods to put in and what foods to leave out. It is important that you check and it may seem frustrating and boring at first but you will eventually get used to it. Imagine all the smiling womrs by the end of it.

  • Paper, junk mail, paper egg cartons, cardboard, dry leaves
  • Vegetable and fruit scraps, bread, pasta, coffee grounds and filters, teabags, dead plant matter from houseplants
  • All organic material will break down, some faster than others
  • Spicy food, chili, onion and garlic
  • Meats, bones, fat and anything oily or greasy.
  • Citrus foods like lemons, limes and oranges
  • Dairy products including butter, sour cream, milk, whole eggs (egg shells are ok) and cheese
